who we are.

Inspired to make a difference, St Andrews Student Incubator brings together creative, entrepenurial-minded students to brainstorm new and exciting start-up ideas and then bring them to life within our own town. Our Incubator will help students to learn vital entrepreneurial and business skills, all the while experiencing a snapshot of the vibrant start-up ecosytem.

what we do.

Throughout the year, our incubator will run workshops and hear from exciting speakers, in order to learn exactly what it takes to bring an idea to life. We encourage every project we develop to have core social/environmental themes within the product or business model. Our vision is to expose more students to start-up career opportunities, while learning how businesses can be essential tools for creating sustainable, positive change within communities.
